Our Story

A Slice of Something Real

Founded in 1998 by Wally Stephens

Rusted Sun Pizzeria was built on one simple idea: great pizza doesn't need to be complicated. We use fresh, quality ingredients and take the time to get it right — every single pie, every single day. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Tucked into Salt Lake City's Granary/Ballpark neighborhood, we're the kind of place where regulars know our names and first-timers leave feeling like they've found a new favorite. Come hungry. Leave happy.

Est. 1998 in SLC Granary District
